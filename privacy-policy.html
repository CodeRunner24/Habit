Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 17, 2025

Habit Bloom (“the App”) values your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Profile Data: optional name, age range, gender.
  • Lifestyle Survey: sleep, stress, activity days, social connection, nutrition.
  • Habit Data: habit names/categories, daily completions, streaks, statistics.
  • Notification Preferences: weekly reminders and AI tips.
  • Subscription Status: purchases and subscriptions managed via the App Store.

2. How We Use Data

  • On-device AI: Core ML models generate insights and habit recommendations directly on your device.
  • Reminders: Notifications are scheduled only if you grant permission.
  • Apple Watch Sync: Data is shared securely between your own devices via Apple’s WatchConnectivity.

3. Data Sharing

  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell your data.

4. Data Storage & Deletion

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • You may reset all data in Settings → Reset All Data, or by deleting the app.

5. Your Rights

  • Manage notifications anytime in iOS Settings.
  • Contact us to request deletion or assistance.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
