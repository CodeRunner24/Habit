FAQ

  • How do I upgrade to Premium?
    From the in-app Upgrade screen. Purchases are handled by the App Store.
  • How do I cancel a free trial?
    Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions and cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends.
  • Notifications don’t work.
    Ensure you allowed notification permissions and check iOS Settings → Notifications → Habit Bloom.
  • How do I delete my data?
    Go to Settings → Reset All Data, or delete the app.
  • How does Apple Watch sync work?
    Data is exchanged securely between your iPhone and Apple Watch only.
  • Where is AI processing done?
    All analysis is done locally on your device using Core ML.
